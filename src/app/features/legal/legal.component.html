Privacy Policy

Last updated: {{ updated }}

SorTrek is a small, invite-based travel planner used by families and friends to organize trips together. This policy explains, in plain language, what data the app stores and why.

What we store

  • Account details — your email address, display name, and (optionally) a profile photo and home city/currency. If you sign in with Google, we receive your name, email, and Google profile picture.
  • Trip content — trips, schedules, bookings, packing lists, expenses, photos, and documents you or your trip members add. This content is shared with the other members of that trip — that is the point of the app.
  • Bank connection (optional) — if you connect a bank through Plaid to import expenses, we store only the Plaid access token needed to fetch your transactions. We never see or store your bank username or password; that is handled entirely by Plaid. You can disconnect at any time from the Costs tab, which deletes the token.
  • Notification tokens — if you enable push notifications, a device token is stored so we can send trip reminders. Disabling notifications removes it.

What we don't do

  • No advertising, no analytics trackers, no selling or sharing of data with third parties.
  • No cookies beyond the essential sign-in session.
  • Gmail import (if used) requests read access through a Google popup for one scan; the access token is never stored on our servers.

Where data lives

Data is stored in Google Firebase (Firestore, Storage, Authentication) and processed by serverless functions on Vercel. AI features send only the trip context you're asking about to the AI provider to generate a response; conversations are not used to train models.

Sharing & visibility

Trip content is visible to the members of that trip. If a trip owner turns on a public itinerary link, the schedule and bookings on that page are visible to anyone with the link until sharing is turned off.

Deleting your data

Profile → Security → Delete account permanently erases your profile, trips you own (and their contents), photos and documents you uploaded anywhere, your Plaid connection, and notification tokens. This is immediate and irreversible.

Contact

Questions? Contact the person who invited you, or the app operator.
